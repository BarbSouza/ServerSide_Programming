About the Game

You start just as a little rabbit with nothing in this big florest. Explore and get resources to build, survive and improve your rabbit. It's a big florest full with secrets which the more places you go the more you know. Play by yourself or with frinds. This Adventure waits for YOU!!

  • Gather Resources Items, PowerUps, Materials;
  • Build Shelter and improve the scenery;
  • Survive Find water and food and fight predators;
  • Explore Find new habitats, new species, new habilities;
  • Create Improve your rabbit, making clothes and other accessories to make your rabbit stronger and cuter.

Play in any platform
