How to Play

Welcome to Monster Mayhem! Here are the instructions on how to play the game:

  • Player 1 and Player 2 take turns selecting hexagons on the game board.
  • Be quick! You have 5 seconds to make a move before the monster gets you.
  • Avoid selecting losing hexagons, indicated by a red color. Selecting one ends the game.
  • The player that scape from the monster wins the game!

    • Good luck and have fun!
